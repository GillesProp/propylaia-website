What MemoJo will do

In simple words: a guided path from life to posterity.

During the user's life, MemoJo gently helps:

  • Gathering the important memories and life experiences.
  • Instilling context and depth.
  • Framing all entries into smart retrievable content.

When the user passes, MemoJo :

  • Transmit the user's recipient-specific memoirs (digital and printed).

    • Long after the user's death, MemoJo keeps on.

    • Trascending death by continuing to deliver time/location trigerred user's messages and letting their story available.

Meet Jo.

Jo is the AI at the heart of MemoJo. Throughout a user‚Äôs life ‚Äî and even after ‚Äî Jo takes on different roles to support them:

  • Guide.
  • Interviewer.
  • Archivist.
  • Book designer.

    • Jo is trained separately from users personal data. Their stories stay theirs: Jo never uses their content to train itself, and nothing is shared outside.

    When a user passes away, Jo becomes their personal library curator. It can naturally answer questions from loved ones, without inventing facts, by retrieving the exact entries connected to each question.
